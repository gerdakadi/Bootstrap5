Inside the Garden...

A peek at the magical things you'll discover

Plant your first luminous seed and watch it shimmer beneath the starlight. Learn to nurture it with moonwater and kindness.

Meet your first garden spirit — a shy foxglow familiar — and discover how to attune with its gentle energy.

Craft glowing lanterns, enchanted soil, and star-woven clothing to thrive in your garden’s soft nights.

Experiment with recipes that change color under different moon phases!

Befriend moon moths, spirit frogs, and tiny stardust bunnies who help you gather rare seeds.

Each creature carries a story. listen closely to unlock hidden melodies of the night.

Unearth forbidden seeds that glow in shades unknown. But beware — not all that blooms is kind.

Balance beauty and danger as you uncover the lore of the midnight soil.

When the moon covers the sun, the entire garden awakens in luminous bloom. Join your familiars in a night of magic and music.

Complete your journey as the true Keeper of the Night Garden.
